Our Commitment to IDEA

Belonging occurs where Inclusion, Diversity, Equity and Access (IDEA) intersect. Since the inception of Girls on the Run in 1996, our goal has always been to create a welcoming and safe space where all of our participants can unlock the potential they have within. Learn more about our organization-wide commitment to IDEA.

IDEA Statement

At Girls on the Run, our core values guide our way. We have deepened our commitment to be a place of belonging that authentically engages, affirms and values all people. We find strength in connectedness and are inspired to lead the organization in a way that m irrors the lessons we teach our girls.

Our IDEA Commission

Girls on the Run International established an IDEA Commission to develop strategic imperatives—the framework for everyone within the Girls on the Run movement—to guide organizational work. Within the commission, there are 7subcommittees, all dedicated to creating tactics and evaluation measures to ensure positive outcomes.
